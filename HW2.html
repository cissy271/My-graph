Uneven distribution of fundamental education resources in China

There has been a great development of literacy rate over China since recent 20 years. However, the development rate is quite different in each province. Comparing the enrolment ratio and graduate ratio of primary school and pupil-teacher ratio, it could be found that coastal areas are more well-resourced than inland areas.
